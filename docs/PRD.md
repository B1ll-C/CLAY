# CLAY — Product Requirements Document

> Status: Draft — to be completed in Phase 4 (Inventory) and Phase 5 (Shopping List)

## Overview

CLAY is an offline-first grocery and inventory management mobile app for individuals and families.

## Goals

- Reduce household food waste through expiration tracking
- Save money through price comparison across stores
- Prevent duplicate purchases through accurate inventory tracking
- Work reliably in low/no connectivity environments
